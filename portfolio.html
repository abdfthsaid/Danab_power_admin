About the Project

Danab Power is a comprehensive power bank rental management system designed to streamline operations and provide real-time insights for business stakeholders.

Advanced analytics and reporting tools to track revenue, monitor trends, and make data-driven business decisions.

Complete control over power bank stations, including real-time status monitoring and inventory management.

User Management
